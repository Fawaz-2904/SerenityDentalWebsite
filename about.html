Who We Are

At Serenity Dental, we combine advanced dental expertise with a calm, boutique experience — redefining what modern dentistry feels like.

Founded by Dr. Adit Shah (MDS Orthodontist) and Dr. Pooja Shah (MDS Prosthodontist & Implantologist), our clinic in Juhu, Mumbai brings together precision, technology, and care in a serene, patient-focused environment.

With over 15 years of specialist experience, our team provides comprehensive dental solutions — from implants and smile design to aligners and restorative care — all under one roof.

Using advanced systems like the iTero® 3D Scanner and modern sterilization protocols, we ensure precision, safety, and comfort at every step.

Beyond technology, we believe great dentistry is deeply personal — focused on your goals, your comfort, and your long-term health.
