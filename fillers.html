Tooth Fillings

Restore decayed or damaged teeth with natural-looking dental fillings designed for strength, comfort, and long-lasting protection.

Restore &
Protect

Dental fillings are used to repair teeth affected by cavities, fractures, or minor wear. Modern tooth-colored fillings blend seamlessly with your natural teeth while restoring their strength and function.

At Serenity Dental, we use advanced composite materials and minimally invasive techniques to preserve healthy tooth structure while delivering durable, aesthetic results that feel completely natural.

Comfortable &
Precise Care

The procedure begins with removing the decayed portion of the tooth, followed by careful cleaning and placement of high-quality filling material to restore shape and durability.

Our filling treatments include:

  • Tooth-colored composite fillings
  • Cavity treatment & restoration
  • Replacement of old fillings
  • Repair of chipped teeth
  • Minimally invasive restorations
  • Preventive decay management
