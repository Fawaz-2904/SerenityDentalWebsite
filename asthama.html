Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about asthma and COPD care services.

Asthma usually involves reversible airway inflammation, while COPD is a progressive lung disease that causes long-term breathing difficulties.

While there is no complete cure, proper treatment and lifestyle management can significantly control symptoms and improve quality of life.

Common triggers include dust, smoke, pollution, pollen, infections, cold air, stress, and certain allergens.

Avoiding triggers, following prescribed medications, maintaining healthy habits, and attending regular checkups can help reduce flare-ups.
